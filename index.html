About Me

I’m a North Carolina native and proud University of North Carolina at Charlotte alum with over 7 years of experience as a data analyst. From the moment I cracked open my first spreadsheet, I found myself hooked on turning messy data into clear, actionable insights. After earning my Bachelor’s degree, I’ve spent my career partnering with teams across industries—driving efficiency, detecting data quality issues, and building dashboards that tell a story at a glance.

My true passion lies in solving complex problems: whether it’s automating a data-quality pipeline, uncovering production anomalies at a wind farm, or creating interactive visualizations that help stakeholders make informed decisions. When I’m not wrangling numbers, you’ll find me exploring the Blue Ridge Mountains, experimenting with home-brewed coffee, or geeking out over the latest Plotly chart types. I’m always excited to connect with fellow data enthusiasts, so feel free to reach out!

Solar Contract Sales & Invoice Automation

Automated Python system that ingests raw contract data, cleans it, auto-generates invoice files from a template, and dashboards revenue performance.

  1. Data Ingestion: Generate or load raw sales/contract CSVs (customer, date, kWh, rate, total).
  2. Data Cleaning: Standardize formats, compute missing totals, flag invoice status.
  3. Invoice Generation: Fill an Excel template with placeholders (ContractID, CustomerName, etc.) to produce per-contract invoice files.
  4. Dashboard: Live Streamlit dashboard showing total contracts, revenue, and monthly revenue trends.

Why It Matters: Simplifies the end‐to‐end sales process—reducing manual effort, ensuring billing accuracy, and providing transparent KPIs.

US Electricity Generation & Stability

Live Streamlit dashboard tracking U.S. electricity mix evolution, with interactive charts of monthly generation by source and renewable share.

  1. Data Acquisition: Fetch monthly generation data via the EIA public API.
  2. Data Cleaning: Convert raw API output to structured CSV, parse dates, and compute shares.
  3. Dashboard: Build interactive charts with Streamlit and Plotly for filtering by source and date.
  4. Deployment: Host on Streamlit Cloud and embed here for live updates.

Why It Matters: Transforms government data into an accessible visualization, highlighting the rise of renewables and seasonal trends in the U.S. power mix.
