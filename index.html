US Electricity Generation & Stability

Interactive, live Streamlit dashboard showing monthly generation by source and renewables share. Click “Full Screen” for best experience.

  1. Gathering the data: We tap into the U.S. Energy Information Administration’s public API to fetch up-to-date monthly figures for each power source.
  2. Cleaning & preparing: A Python script reads the raw data, converts month codes into dates, calculates each source’s share of total generation, and outputs a clean CSV.
  3. Building the dashboard: Using Streamlit and Plotly, we load the cleaned data, let visitors filter by source and date, and draw interactive charts directly in their browser.
  4. Publishing & embedding: The app runs on Streamlit Cloud and is seamlessly embedded here via an iframe, always showing the latest results.

Why it matters: This project turns raw government data into an intuitive, shareable experience, highlighting seasonal trends and the rapid growth of clean energy in the U.S. electricity mix.
